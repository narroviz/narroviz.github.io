About Us

Founded in 2020 by David Newcomb, Wanderwhim is a creative data visualization space built to explore all sorts of topics and datasets. The site was bootstrapped using open source templates provided by The Pudding and excellent educational resources such as Amelia Wattenberger's Fullstack D3 & Data Visualization.

In the early stages, we expect to release about 1 article per month and grow from there. If you want to help support the work being done at Wanderwhim, please visit Patreon to become a supporter. For just $1/month, you can help us create insightful and beautiful data stories!

If you want to collaborate with us, hire us for a data story, or support us in any other way, please reach out to hello@wanderwhim.com!
